\subsection{Elementära primitiva funktioner (p.281)}

\[
    \int \frac{1}{1 + x^2}\ dx = \arctan x + C.
\]

Lösning:

\begin{align*}
    \int_0^1 \frac{1}{1 + x^2} dx &= \left[ \arctan x \right]_0^1\\
    &= (\arctan 1) - (\arctan 0)\\
    &= \frac{\pi}{4} - 0\\
    &= \frac{\pi}{4}
\end{align*}

Svar: $\frac{\pi}{4}$
